# Logical Operators

| Operator | Definition                           | Example               |
|----------|--------------------------------------|-----------------------|
| **and**      | True if both statements are true     | 5 > 3 and 9 < 12      |
| **or**       | True if one of the statement is true | 5 > 3 or 9 > 12       |
| **not**      | Reverse resultat                     | not (5 > 3 or 9 > 12) |
